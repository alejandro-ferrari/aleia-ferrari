# Optional — let your current AI pre-fill your profile

If you already use ChatGPT or Claude, you can let it summarize what it knows about you so
onboarding is faster and richer. Paste the block below into THAT assistant, **review the
output and delete anything you don't want to share**, then paste its answer back into Cowork.

```text
Based on what you know about me from our past conversations and memory, fill in the profile
below for a work/productivity assistant. ONLY include work/productivity-relevant facts.
Do NOT include health, finances, legal matters, family or relationship details, home address,
credentials, or any private information about other people. For each line, tag it
[fact] / [preference] / [inference] and add (low / med / high confidence). If you don't know a
field, leave it blank — do not guess.

- Name:
- What I do (role / work / main projects):
- Tools and AI assistants I use:
- My top recurring tasks or pain points:
- How I like an assistant to work with me (tone, level of detail, push-back):
- What a useful daily brief would include for me:
- Timezone and preferred language:

Return only the filled list.
```

> Privacy note: this shares part of what your AI knows about you. You stay in control —
> review and trim before pasting it back. Aleia never stores sensitive data.
